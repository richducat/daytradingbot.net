What Bluechip does

It watches. It checks. It waits unless everything lines up.

Bluechip looks for a 1.5% pullback in AAPL, NVDA, TSLA, SPY, QQQ, AMD, MSFT, and GOOGL. A price drop alone is not enough. It also checks the current price, what you already own, orders that are still waiting, and the dollar limits you chose.

Connect on your Mac

Your Robinhood sign-in stays with Robinhood.

In the DayTradingBot Mac app, press Connect Robinhood. Robinhood opens its own approval screen and asks you to choose a dedicated Agentic account. After you approve it, Bluechip can read that account and place only the trades you allow. DayTradingBot never asks for your Robinhood password and cannot transfer or withdraw your money.

Your trading money stays in the Robinhood Agentic account you control. DayTradingBot is software, not a broker or a place to deposit money.

Straight answers

Know the difference before you use real money.

Does Practice predict what I will earn?

No. Practice shows what the rule would do with current information. It cannot predict the price you would receive later or whether a real trade would gain or lose money.

Does a dollar limit prevent every loss?

No. Limits cap how much Bluechip may add to new trades. They cannot prevent a stock from falling or promise that you will get the exact price shown.

Does Bluechip sell stocks?

The launch strategy is focused on small market buys when its checks pass. Do not treat Bluechip as a complete investing plan or assume it manages every position for you.

Can I use the browser app instead?

You can use the browser for onboarding, your access code, Practice, and account information. The Mac app keeps the Robinhood Agentic trading connection on your computer.
